I am a Ph.D Student in Psychology in the "Neuropsychology and Imaging of Human Memory" unit (NIHM) in the University of Caen (France). My researches focus on the function of forgetting, both from neural and historical points of view.


Background

I completed studies in both Philosophy and Applied Mathematics in Grenoble (UPMF). I obtained a Master’s degree in Cognitive science at the University of Lyon 2 where I specialised in Philosophy of Mind (topics: phenomenal consciousness and the epistemological foundation of neuropsychoanalysis). After that, I completed a second Master's degree in the University of Caen with a specialization in neuropsychology (topics: sleep and prospective memory).
